About Me

Hi there, welcome to my fisrt portfolio website! I'm Fikra Ulya a psychology student also who works as a part-timer illustrator. I'm currently learning in a website development and UI/UX program.

I am someone who has an interest in design and illustration. My interest in design has been since I was in high school. I hope you all enjoy my work!
